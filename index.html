MEET ODIE

BY NATE ARRIOLA

For those unfamiliar with Odie, he is a 21-year old r&b singer from San Deigo. ODIE is a rising start, releasing mainly singles until 2018 where he dropped Analogue an album that showcases his talent and potential. Although a rookie to the music industry, he undoubtedly became one of my favorite artist of 2018. Everything about Odie is refreshing to me and genuinely exciting. His songwriting, production, and creativity are consistently immersive - giving you a roller coaster of emotions with each song. So, I highly recommend you experience this for yourself, and give the rising king a listen on the musical platform of your choice : ).
